About Me - Adianna Barrett

General information about the writer of the website and why the website was created.

Hi! My name is Adianna Barrett and I am senior Legal Studies and English Major with a certificate in Professional Writing an Technical Communication, and specialization in Constitutional Law. I am currently in English 381 working on this MadCap Flare Project.

In my time at UMass I have been all over the map. I first started out as an education major thinking I was going to teach High School, but I soon ended up learning that did not align to my interests. I knew I loved writing and wanted to strengthen this area, so I took Shakespeare and English 200, therefore changing my major to English. I decided to add Legal Studies as my primary after taking Legal 250. I wanted to help the general public understand their rights and overall serve the community. I decided to stay with both majors in order to strengthen my legal and writing knowledge to become a Paralegal. I intend to take a gap year in order to travel and focus on getting into a good Law School.

The reasoning behind doing these classes, was because I felt as though I learned more about how to write in PWTC than any class I had been taking at UMass. These classes let me hone in my skills and also let me work on something I am passionate about which is web design and coding. I really loved these classes since it gave me an insight of things we don't normally learn about: accessibility, technical writing, web design, and practically our own interests.

If you read this far, thank you so much for so many amazing semesters -- it was really nice to have a teacher who cared about letting the students be themselves, while also strengthening so many of our personal skills.
