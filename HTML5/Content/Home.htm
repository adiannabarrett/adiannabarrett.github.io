Frequently Asked Questions

How do you check if your website is accessible?

You can check if your website is accessible by using the WAVE checker. This is explained in the Accessibility page.

Why did you pick Alt Text? 

I picked Alt Text because before PWTC classes, I did not know much about it and wanted to look into more. I think every time I go onto a website and it does not load, there is never any update to include something so simple as Alt Text. It is a universal idea that should be added always.

What can you take with you from this website?

Good question. I think you can take away that Accessibility matters always, and it is good to be conscious and aware when you do your own projects. Even something so simple as a presentation and including contrast, alt text, hierarchy, etc., can go a long way.

Regarding Unity OnDemand, you can take away the basic information on what it is and how to use it according to the video. It is a great platform to grow your software skills.
